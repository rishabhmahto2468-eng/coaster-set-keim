About.

Hey there!

We're all about making cool coaster sets that not only shield your tables but also jazz up your place with a bit of charm and personality.

We think even something small, like a coaster, can say a lot about you. It's not just a spot for your drink; it shows you care about the little stuff, like being a good host and savoring life's simple joys.

✨ Our Story

So, we got into this because we dig making stuff and appreciate cool design. It started with being fascinated by how things feel, what they're made of, and the knack for handmade stuff. Before long, we wanted to make things that look good.

In a world where everything's made super fast, we wanted to chill out and bring back the joy of owning things made with a purpose.

It hit us:

It's the little things that make life rad.

That's why we started whipping up coasters that look good and do their job. We mix natural stuff, neat patterns, and clever designs.

Over the years, we've grown, always keeping in mind that things should look awesome and make a statement without being too loud.

🪵 What We Make

Each coaster set is made with lots of care, skill, and good materials. Our folks work together to turn basic stuff into slick pieces.

🌲 What We Use

We play around with all types of materials.

Wood — Always feels right.

Cork — Great for soaking up spills and good for the earth.

Ceramic &amp; Stone — Works with fancy and simple setups.

Resin &amp; Epoxy — You can make them pretty wild.

Leather &amp; Faux Leather — Classy.

Metal — For modern spaces.

Silicone &amp; Rubber — Good for outside or with kids.

Everything gets cut, smoothed, and checked to make sure it's perfect.

We go for balance, so they feel good in your hand and look great on your table.
