Home
Coasters are those little flat things you put under your drinks to keep them from messing up your tables. They're good for:

*   Stopping water rings, heat marks, and scratches on your furniture
*   Soaking up the drips from cold drinks
*   Making your place look a little nicer – whether it's your house, the office, or a coffee shop

**What They're Made Of**

Different stuff gives them different vibes and works better for different things:

| Material          | What's Cool About It                                   | Best For                     |
| :---------------- | :----------------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------- |
| Wood              | Lasts long, looks kinda rustic, can handle hot stuff     | Coffee tables, dinner rooms  |
| Cork              | Light, soaks up spills, good for the planet              | Everyday use at home         |
| Ceramic / Stone   | Fancy, heavy, can take the heat, often has cool designs | Nice parties or decorations |
| Silicone / Rubber | Bendy, doesn't slide, easy to clean                    | Outside, kids' rooms         |
| Leather           | Looks classy, doesn't get too wet                       | Offices, bars                |
| Resin             | Artsy, you can make them how you want, water-proof       | Gifts, modern-looking places |
| Metal             | Strong, shiny, sleek                                   | Bars, industrial-style rooms |

**What You Get in a Set**

Usually, a coaster set comes with:

*   4, 6, or 8 coasters
*   Maybe a little holder to keep them stacked

Sometimes they throw in matching placemats or those little drink tags to be extra fancy.

**Shapes and Designs**

Coasters come in all sorts of designs:

*   Shapes: Round (most common), square, hexagon, oval, or whatever shape you want
*   Styles: Simple, with pictures, carved, painted, or with a theme (holidays, nature, etc.)
*   Custom: You can get them with your name, a company logo, or some artwork

**How to Clean Them**

| Material          | How to Clean                                       |
| :---------------- | :------------------------------------------------- |
| Wood / Cork       | Wipe with a damp cloth, don't soak them           |
| Ceramic / Stone   | Wash gently by hand, no strong cleaners          |
| Silicone / Rubber | Throw them in the dishwasher or wash by hand       |
| Leather           | Wipe with a dry or slightly damp cloth            |
| Resin             | Use a soft cloth, don't use anything scratchy     |

Tip: Keep your coasters dry and in their holder so they don't get moldy or dusty.

**More Than Just for Drinks**

You can also use coasters for:

*   Putting under candles, plants, or little statues to decorate
*   Holding your jewelry or keys
*   Making art projects

**Good for Gifts**

Coaster sets are great for:

*   Housewarming presents
*   Gifts from companies
*   Weddings or anniversaries
*   Holidays

You can get them personalized with names, photos, or designs to make them really special.

**Good for the Earth**

If you care about the planet, look for coasters made from:

*   Cork, bamboo, or recycled wood
*   Packaging that breaks down easily
*   Ones made by hand locally

**How Big Are They?**

*   Round: About 3.5 to 4 inches across
*   Square: About 4x4 inches
*   How thick they are depends on what they're made of.
